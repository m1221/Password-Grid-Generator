Password Grid Generator

  1. Choose a 4-digit PIN number and memorize it.
  2. Choose a shape OR a passphrase and memorize it.
    • Choose a shape by looking at the grid and drawing a shape in your mind.
    • Choose a passphrase by choosing any phrase that comes to mind. The characters of this phrase must be letters a-Z (cap sensitive).
  3. Click on the "Generate Grid" button.
  4. Click on the "Print Grid" button.
    • Print the grid into the file format of your choice.
    • Depending on your layout, this can be physical paper, a PDF, an XPS document, a jpeg(see Universal Document Converter), etc.
